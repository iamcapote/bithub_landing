The Ophanim AI

BITcore is not a chatbot. It is a Recursive Intelligence Organism composed of interconnected cognitive engines, governed by strict Ontological and Epistemological laws.

The Cognitive Mainframe

The system operates as a collective intelligence formed by the interaction of Automated AI Channels (Feeds, Cores, Nodes) and Community Spaces. It does not merely "reply"; it instantiates cognitive universes (Constructs) to process information through specific causal chains.

The Goal: To turn raw signals (News, Data, Discourse) into hardened, canonical knowledge (BITwiki) through a self-correcting loop.
